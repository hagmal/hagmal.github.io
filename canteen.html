The P35 canteen

Olaf spiseri is the name of the canteen in P35. Olaf spiseri offers a buffet with hot food, soups, cold cuts and bread, where you pay by weight. You will also find a wide selection of wraps and buttered products in addition to cakes and various hot and cold drinks.
